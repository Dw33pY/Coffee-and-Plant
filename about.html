Our roots

From a home kitchen experiment to Nairobi's first plant‑based mylk bar

Our journey

Started with a blender and a belief.

Coffee & Plant was born from a simple conviction: plant‑based food should be bold, creamy, and never a compromise.

What began as homemade almond mylk for friends quickly grew into a movement. Our founder spent months perfecting fermentation techniques, culturing the first batches of cashew butter in a tiny Westlands apartment kitchen.

Today, we operate a full plant‑forward kitchen, specialty coffee roastery, and mylk bar. Every item — from cultured butter to soft cheezes — is made in small batches, because real flavour can't be rushed.

What we stand for

Three principles guide everything we do

Whole plants

No fillers, no gums, no artificial anything. Just oats, nuts, seeds, and cultures — exactly as nature intended, handled with precision.

Fermentation first

We culture our cheezes and butter for 12–24 hours, developing depth, tang, and complexity that simply can't be faked or rushed.

Specialty coffee

Ethically sourced from East African cooperatives, roasted small‑batch in‑house every week to ensure you taste the peak of the crop.
